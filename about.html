ABOUT CoPED

Established in March 2009, CoPED is an initiative to make UUM the center for computing professional trainings and certifications aiming to cater its service to Northern Malaysia and beyond. CoPED, being an established training center, have conducted more than 100 trainings, certifications, and testings. We are located at School of Computing, Universiti Utara Malaysia.

We offer a wide range of computing courses from basic trainings to advance levels and up to professional certifications. Like other profesional certifications, computing certification has been regarded as an assurance or endorsement of qualification that positively reflect an individual's professional ability to perform a job/task or having the required experience/skill. Studies have shown that various benefits flourished a company hiring or having professionally certified personnel whether in terms of increased productivity, effectiveness, efficiency, or customer satisfaction.
